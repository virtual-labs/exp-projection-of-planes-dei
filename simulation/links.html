PROJECTION OF PLANES

Choose any of the following problems

  1. A Square plane with a 70mm side has its surface parallel to and 30mm above the HP. Draw its projections, when: (a) A side is parallel to VP (b) A side is inclined at 30⁰ to VP and (c) All sides are equally inclined to VP.
  2. A pentagonal plane with a 70mm side has an edge on the HP, the surface of the plane is inclined at 45⁰ to the HP. Draw it’s projections.
  3. An equilateral triangular plane whose side is 90mm is kept in such a way that its surface is perpendicular to both HP and VP and base is parallel to the VP. Draw its projections.
  4. A Square plane ABCD of side 30 mm, rests on its corner in HP. The surface of the plane is inclined at 45⁰ to the XY line such that its diagonal is parallel to the HP and inclined at 30⁰ to the VP. Draw its projections.
